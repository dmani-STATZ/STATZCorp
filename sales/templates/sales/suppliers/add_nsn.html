What is Match Score?

Match Score determines how prominently this supplier appears on solicitations for a given NSN. Scores are calculated automatically from contract history — you do not need to set them manually.

Every NSN you add here gives the supplier a base score of 1.0. If the supplier has won contracts for that NSN in the past, additional points are added automatically: 1.0 per contract won within 2 years, 0.75 for 2–4 years ago, and 0.5 for older contracts. Suppliers with real contract history will always rank above suppliers added manually with no history.
