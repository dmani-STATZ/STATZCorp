The Contract Review page's Packaging section is now **Contract Level Charges**, listing every charge on the contract (Packaging, GSI Fee, Freight, etc.) with supplier, estimated, and billed/paid amounts. Legacy packaging records still appear beneath the charges while they're being converted.

The Split Summary section now shows each company's split percentage and an expandable breakdown — per-CLIN values with packaging and charge deductions — matching the Finance Audit page, so the numbers reconcile at a glance.
