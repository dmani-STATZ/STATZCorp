Supplier flat fees — such as GCI fees, GSI fees, packaging charges, and freight — can
now be entered and tracked as Supplier Charges directly on the contract, alongside
regular CLINs.

**What changed:**

- Supplier Charges appear in the CONTRACT LINE ITEMS list on Contract Management.
  Click a charge row to view and edit its details (type, supplier, quote amount, paid
  amount, payment date, invoice number) using the standard field-edit experience.
- Supplier Charges appear inline in the CLIN table on Finance Audit, with clickable
  Quote and Paid amounts.
- Charges entered during Intake now flow automatically into the Purchase Order as
  line items, giving Barb full visibility when generating supplier POs.
- A **+ Add Charge** button on Contract Management allows charges to be added or
  corrected after award without going back through Intake.
- All field edits are tracked through the Transactions audit system.
