**Processing — Plan Gross Calculation Updated**

Packaging (packhouse) quote amounts are no longer deducted from Plan Gross during contract processing. Packaging costs are now handled by the Finance team. The Packhouse Quote field remains available for data capture and is still copied to the contract on finalization.
