**Acknowledgment Letter filename updated** — The PDF saved to the contract's SharePoint folder is now named `Purchase Order Acknowledgment Letter.pdf` (previously `{po}-purchase-order-acknowledge-letter.pdf`). Letters already saved under the old name will not load automatically on page load — simply click **Send to Contract Folder** to re-save under the new filename.
