STATZWeb now monitors the DIBBS public website for DLA notices — including CMMC compliance updates, PAR code changes, and cybersecurity announcements — and surfaces them on the portal home page. New notices (posted within the last 7 days) are highlighted automatically. No more missing important DLA communications buried at the bottom of DIBBS.
