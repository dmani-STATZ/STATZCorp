The **Award Ledger** now has its own page. Open it from the **Award Ledger** button in the Intake Queue header, or go straight to `/intake/ledger/`.

The page mirrors the DIBBS awards we saw for your CAGEs and overlays each award's intake lifecycle, so you can answer — per contract — what we saw, whether we won it, whether and when a draft was created and worked, how much mod activity there was, and whether and when it became a live contract. It is the go-to surface for spotting won awards that never entered intake.

- **Filter** by lifecycle state, we-won status, CAGE, a free-text search (contract number, award/basic number, purchase request, or NSN), and a "first seen" date range.
- **Sort** by contract number, award date, total price, or lifecycle stage, and page through results 50 at a time — your filters and sort carry across pages.
- **Export CSV** of exactly what you're looking at (all current filters applied) with one click.
- The page is **read-only** and automatically scoped to your company's CAGEs, so you only ever see — and export — your own awards.
