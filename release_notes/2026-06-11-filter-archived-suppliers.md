Processing:
Archived suppliers no longer appear in supplier search results when matching
a CLIN or assigning a packhouse during contract processing. This applies to the
standard CLIN supplier modal, the packhouse assignment modal, and the IDIQ
inline supplier picker.

Intake:
Intake supplier match search now excludes archived suppliers. Archived
suppliers no longer appear as selectable options in the match modal for
CLINs, packaging, and IDIQ pairs.
