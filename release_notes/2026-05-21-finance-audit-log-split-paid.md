## Finance Audit — Log Split Paid

Jenny can now record split paid amounts directly from the Finance Audit page. Click the **Split Paid** header in the Split Summary card to open the **Log Split Paid** modal. Enter the total paid for each company and save — the system distributes the amount proportionally across CLINs automatically. A warning highlights any discrepancy between what was paid and the expected split value.
