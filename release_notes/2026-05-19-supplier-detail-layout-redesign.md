**Supplier detail page: new two-column layout with sidebar navigation.**

The supplier detail page now uses a fixed left sidebar for quick section jumps and supplier search, with the main report scrolling on the right. Sidebar links highlight automatically as you scroll through Overview, Compliance & Status, Contacts, Addresses, Contracts, Certifications & Classifications, Files & Documents, and Notes.

**Jump search:** Type at least two characters in the sidebar to find another supplier by name, CAGE, or contract number and open their detail page without returning to the dashboard.

**Status warnings:** Probation, conditional, and archived suppliers show prominent banners at the top of Overview when those flags are set.

**Prime field:** Prime now shows a readable sales team label when available, or an em dash instead of a raw numeric ID.
