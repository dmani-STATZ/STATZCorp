## Partner Commission Reconciliation

Jenny can now upload a partner's commission report (Excel) and automatically
compare it against STATZWeb's split records. The reconciliation flags:

- **Amount discrepancies** — their claimed commission vs. what we have on record
- **Payment discrepancies** — they say paid but we haven't recorded payment (or vice versa)
- **Missing in STATZ** — contracts they listed that we can't find
- **Missing in partner report** — contracts we have splits for that they didn't include

Results can be exported to Excel for sharing or record-keeping.

Access via **Contracts → Partner Reconciliation**.
