The supplier detail page has been restructured to reduce wasted space and add
visual depth:

- **Supplier header and health gauge** now sit side by side at the top of the page
- **Contact & Web and Compliance & Status** share a row, putting more information
  above the fold
- **Certifications and Classifications** are locked side by side and no longer wrap
- **Contacts** are now rendered as individual cards with a left-border color system
  based on the first letter of the contact's name (A–F blue, G–L teal, M–R amber,
  S–Z purple). The designated primary contact displays a red border and a
  **Primary** badge.
