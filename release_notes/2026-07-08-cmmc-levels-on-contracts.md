**CMMC Levels on Contracts** — Intake now auto-detects CMMC requirements
(Level 1, Level 2 Self-Assessment, Level 2 C3PAO, Level 3) while scanning new
contracts and surfaces them as a CMMC badge on the contract page. A contract can
carry more than one level at once. The four levels are also click-to-toggle on the
contract page, so older contracts can be tagged by hand. The legacy NIST button has
been retired.
