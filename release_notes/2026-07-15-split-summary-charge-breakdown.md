The Split Summary breakdown on the Finance Audit page now lists each contract-level charge (Packaging, GSI Fee, Freight, etc.) as its own deduction line under each company, so the expanded CLIN breakdown adds up to the company split total. Charges paid to a specific supplier show the supplier name alongside the charge label.
