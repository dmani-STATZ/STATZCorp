The Contract Details section of the IDIQ draft editor now shows only fields relevant to IDIQ contracts: **Contract Number**, **Buyer**, **Award Date**, and **Files URL**.

Fields not tracked on IDIQ contracts (Due Date, Contract Value, PR Number, Plan Gross, Planned Split, NIST, PO Number) are hidden for IDIQ drafts. They remain visible for all other contract types (AWD, PO, DO, etc.).
