STATZWeb now monitors session activity and notifies you before your session expires.

- A warning appears 5 minutes before your session times out, with a live countdown and a **Stay Connected** button to renew it without losing your work.
- If your session expires, a lock screen prevents further edits and offers a **Log In in a New Tab** option  so you can re-authenticate and return to save any open forms without losing data.
- Background activity checks (unread message counter) are automatically paused after 15 minutes of inactivity so your session can expire naturally when you walk away.
