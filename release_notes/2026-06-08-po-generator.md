Companies with the PO Generator enabled now have access to a "Generate PO" button on the contract management page. 

The purchase order page automatically seeds a line item for each CLIN on the contract, supports adding, editing, deleting, and reordering lines, and allows inserting saved snippet library text. A dedicated print view supports exporting the purchase order to PDF directly through the browser's print engine.
