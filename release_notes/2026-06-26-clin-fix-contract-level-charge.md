The CLIN Fix tool's "Convert to Packaging" option has been replaced with
the more general **Convert to Contract Level Cost**. When reclassifying a
legacy CLIN, you can now choose the charge label (Packaging, GSI Fee,
Freight, or a custom label via Other) and optionally update the supplier
via autocomplete search. All other behavior — income-side guard, notes
migration, audit log — is unchanged.
