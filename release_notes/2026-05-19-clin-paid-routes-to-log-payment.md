On the Finance Audit page, clicking **Paid** or **Customer Pay** on a CLIN 
row now opens the Log Payment modal with that CLIN pre-selected. If the CLIN 
has shipments, you will be prompted to select which shipment to log the 
payment against — keeping payments tied to the correct shipment and ensuring 
they roll up to the CLIN automatically. CLINs without shipments log directly 
as before. Quote Value and Item Value cells are unchanged.
