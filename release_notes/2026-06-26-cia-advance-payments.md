Finance Audit now supports **CIA (Cash-In-Advance) supplier payments** alongside existing contract-level charges. When adding a contract action, choose **Charge** (existing behavior — deducts from adj gross) or **CIA Advance** (pre-payment to supplier — tracked separately, does not affect adj gross).

CIA advance rows appear in **orange** in the Finance Audit CLIN table with a **CIA** badge. Their paid amount counts toward the supplier Paid column total so Finance can see money out at a glance.
