The Adj Gross figure in the Contract Summary card on the Finance Audit page now reflects actual CLIN financial data rather than the manually entered Plan Gross.

Previously, Contract Adj Gross was calculated as Plan Gross minus Finance Costs minus Packaging. This meant the number was only as accurate as the Plan Gross entry and did not automatically reflect what was actually entered at the CLIN level.

The new formula is the sum of all CLIN Adj Gross values (which include each CLIN's income, supplier costs, and finance line deductions) minus the packaging cost. Plan Gross remains visible on the page as a planning benchmark — the number you expected to make — so you can compare it against the actual Adj Gross at a glance.
