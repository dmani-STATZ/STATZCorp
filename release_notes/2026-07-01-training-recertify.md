You can now recertify a completed training course at any time from **My Requirements** — you no longer have to wait until it expires. For courses that don't require a certificate, click **Recertify Early** (or **Recertify Now** once expired) to log a fresh completion date. For courses that require a certificate, upload your new certificate — that upload is what recertifies you, so no certificate means no recertification for those courses. Your prior uploaded certificates are always preserved, never overwritten.
