You can now recertify a completed training course at any time from **My Requirements** — you no longer have to wait until it expires. For courses that don't require a certificate, click **Click to Certify** to log a fresh completion date. For courses that require a certificate, use **Upload to Certify** — that upload is what recertifies you. If you just need to fix a file you uploaded by mistake, use **Replace Document** instead — it swaps the file without changing your certification date. Your prior certificates are always preserved, never overwritten.
