On the Finance Audit page, clicking an **Item Value** cell now opens the Payment History ledger
instead of the single-field edit modal. You can log multiple entries — commissions, costs, or
sub-charges — with dates and notes, and the total is automatically summed and saved as the
CLIN's Item Value.

Quote Value continues to use the direct-edit modal.
