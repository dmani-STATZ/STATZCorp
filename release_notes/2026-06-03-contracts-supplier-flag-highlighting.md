Supplier names on the Contract Management CLIN table, CLIN detail card, and Dashboard Recent Contracts table are now highlighted when a supplier is flagged.

- **Probation** — red chip
- **Conditional** — yellow chip

Probation takes priority if both flags are set. Flags are only shown after the supplier has been matched to a live Supplier record — unmatched (raw text) CLINs are unaffected.
