**Processing — Plan Gross Calculation Updated**

Packaging (packhouse) quote amounts are now deducted from Plan Gross at contract entry. This reflects costs that were known and accounted for at bid time. Actual packaging costs and any unexpected cost variances will surface on the Finance Audit page via Adjusted Gross. Contracts without a packaging quote are unaffected — a blank packhouse quote is treated as zero.
