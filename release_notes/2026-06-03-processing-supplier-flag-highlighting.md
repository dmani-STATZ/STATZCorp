Supplier names in the CLIN accordion summary rows and matched-supplier indicators now show a red chip when the matched supplier is on Probation, and a yellow chip when Conditional. Flags are only shown after the supplier has been matched to a live Supplier record — unmatched (raw text) CLINs are unaffected.
