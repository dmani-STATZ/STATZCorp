**Supplier primary contacts**

Primary contacts for suppliers are now managed directly on each contact record. Multiple contacts per supplier can be marked as primary. The primary contact designation drives PO Acknowledgment Letter pre-fill and the Supplier Info panel.

A **Primary Contacts** group is now automatically maintained for each supplier. It always reflects whichever contacts are marked as Primary, updates instantly when the Primary designation is toggled, and can be used for RFQ email and group copy just like any other contact group.
