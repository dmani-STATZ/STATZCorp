**Finance Audit — Log Payment in one place**

A new **+ Log Payment** button in the CLINs header lets you log Customer Pay or Paid amounts without hunting through per-CLIN controls. Select a CLIN, select a shipment, fill in the payment details and save. If the CLIN has no shipments yet, the modal creates one automatically with sensible defaults — just enter the qty and you're done. All totals update instantly without a page reload (unless a new shipment was created).
