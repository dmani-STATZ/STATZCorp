You can now copy every email address for a contact category on a supplier in one click.

On the Supplier Detail page, a new **Copy Category** dropdown sits next to **Add Contact**. Pick a category (Sales, Contracts, Leadership, Finance, etc.) and every contact assigned to that category on the supplier has their email copied to your clipboard — semicolon-separated and ready to paste into an email's To or Cc field.

- Categories with no contacts assigned show up grayed out.
- Contacts missing an email address are skipped, and you'll get a quick heads-up if that happens.
- Duplicate email addresses are combined automatically.
