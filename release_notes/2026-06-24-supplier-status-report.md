A **Probation & Conditional Status Report** button has been added to the Supplier
Dashboard. Clicking it opens a print-ready report in a new browser window listing
all active suppliers on Probation or Conditional status, including their primary
point(s) of contact, date added to the list, and notes. The report can be printed
or saved as a PDF directly from the browser.
