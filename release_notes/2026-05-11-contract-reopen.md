title: Re-open Closed and Canceled Contracts
published: true
publish_date: 2026-05-11

critical: false

You can now re-open a contract that has been Closed or Canceled. On a Closed or Canceled contract, open the **Options** menu and choose **Open Contract**. You'll be asked for a reason — this is recorded in the contract's new **Status History** section along with who re-opened it and when.

The Options menu on Closed and Canceled contracts now shows only the actions that make sense for those states (Finance Audit and Open Contract). Once a contract is re-opened, the full menu returns.

Every contract now also has a **Status History** card on its detail page showing every status change — from initial creation through every close, cancel, and re-open — with timestamps, users, and reasons. History for contracts that existed before this release has been backfilled from the data we had on file.
