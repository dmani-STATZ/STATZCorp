Cori/Jenny can now manage miscellaneous contract-level charges:
- **Processing Form - Contract Charges:** Added a new "Contract Charges" section where you can enter known estimated charges (like Freight, GSI Fee, etc.) at bid time. These charges reduce the calculated Plan Gross value.
- **Finance Audit - Contract Charges:** Estimated charges appear on the Finance Audit page, allowing you to enter the actual billed/paid amount. The variance is computed and colored automatically (green for under budget/savings, red for over budget).
- **Recalculate Splits:** Recalculating company splits now correctly accounts for contract packaging and contract-level charges, ensuring that split totals match the overall Adjusted Gross of the contract.
