On the processing contract form, **Packhouse** and **Contract Charges** are now independent side-by-side sections in a 50/50 layout. Each section has its own collapsed/expanded toggle. The **+ Add Contract Charge** button has been removed from the Contract Actions sidebar — use the section toggle instead.
