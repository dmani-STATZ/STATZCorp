**Save & Finalize** button — analysts can now finalize a draft in a single
click directly from the editor action bar, without the intermediate "Mark
Ready for Review → reopen from queue → Finalize" two-step. The existing
two-step review flow remains fully intact for contracts requiring a
second-person review.
