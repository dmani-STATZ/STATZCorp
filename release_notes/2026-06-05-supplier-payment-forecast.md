A new Payment Forecast page shows what STATZ owes suppliers and when, based on
ship dates and each term's net days. Shipped items show their real due date and
outstanding balance; not-yet-shipped items appear as projections off the target
ship date. Record supplier payments right from the page (same ledger as Finance
Audit), schedule a planned pay date, flag items on hold, and fix a wrong payment
term inline. Items missing a term or target date are surfaced separately so they
can be corrected.
