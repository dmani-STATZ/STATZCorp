STATZ Corporation is your Friendly and Knowledgeable "Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business". Our SDVOSB status has been verified by the VA-CVE and we focus on delivering superior supply chain support to the government, military and commercial entities by providing quality, efficient and cost effective solutions to achieve procurement objectives.

MISSION STATEMENT:

Our mission is to be the best SDVOSB provider of goods and services in markets that we serve.

While we specialize in the government procurement SDVOSB set-aside market of Simplified Acquisition Threshold parts and components, we have a proven track record and demonstrated ability to provide quality service and delivery in all acquisition categories.

We can help you meet your procurement goals for SDVOSB awards and beyond!

OUR HISTORY:

STATZ Corporation was founded as an SDVOSB in 2004 by Bill Statz in Cross Plains, WI. In that time, STATZ Corp has grown to become the largest SDVOSB, and largest small business government contractor in Wisconsin for 2019 with Prime contracts valued at $55 Million dollars. Bill Statz retired in December 2019.

January 2019, Mark Lyons became the new Owner and president of STATZ Corporation. Mark served 20 years active duty where he was a Cavalry Scout, Paratrooper, Jumpmaster and traveled the world for this great nation. Mark looks forward to improving STATZ corporation our our real customers. The War-fighters.
